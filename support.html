Common Questions

My subscription isn't activating

Restore your purchase from Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. If the issue persists, email us with your Apple ID and we'll look into it.

HealthKit data isn't syncing

Go to iPhone Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Run Fueled and confirm all requested permissions are turned on.

Live Activity isn't showing on my lock screen

Make sure Live Activities are enabled for Run Fueled in iPhone Settings → Live Activities. Start a run and check your lock screen — the activity appears once the run begins.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account inside the app. This permanently removes all your data from our servers.
