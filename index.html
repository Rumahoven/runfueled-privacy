Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 8, 2026

Run Fueled ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Run Fueled mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over your data. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Summary: We collect only what is necessary to deliver personalized endurance nutrition guidance. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the App.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID email address (or the private relay address Apple assigns if you choose to hide your email). We store this email linked to your account in our database.

Body and performance metrics. To calculate personalized fuel recommendations, you voluntarily provide:

  • Body weight
  • Biological sex
  • Fitness level
  • Sweat rate and sweat sodium concentration
  • Stomach sensitivity and caffeine tolerance
  • Menstrual cycle phase (optional, only if you enable cycle-phase tracking)

Run and activity data. When you log a run or use Race Day Mode, we collect:

  • Distance, pace, and duration
  • Ambient temperature and humidity
  • Terrain type and fatigue level
  • Pre-run nutrition state
  • Fuel events (timing and product of each gel, drink, electrolyte, or salt intake)
  • Race name (if provided)

Nutrition and product data. We store the fuel products you add to your pantry, your product preferences (favorites, loadout, ratings), and any carb-loading day plans you create.

Gamification data. We store your XP total, level, prestige tier, and achievement progress to power the gamification features of the App.

App usage and feedback. If you submit a bug report or feature request through the App, we collect the text of your submission along with basic device information (iOS version, device model) to diagnose issues. Submissions are rate-limited to three per type per day.

Push notification tokens. If you grant notification permission, we store a notification token on-device to schedule local fuel reminders. We do not send push notifications from our servers except to update Live Activities on your lock screen.

Automatically collected data. We do not run analytics SDKs or third-party tracking. We do not collect IP addresses, advertising identifiers (IDFA), or browsing behavior.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate personalized carbohydrate, sodium, and fluid targets for your runs
  • To deliver Race Day Mode countdowns and real-time fuel event reminders
  • To generate Carb Loading Plans based on your race distance and body weight
  • To synchronize your data across devices when you reinstall the App
  • To maintain your XP, level, and achievement history
  • To process your subscription through Apple's in-app purchase system
  • To respond to feedback submissions and improve the App

We do not use your data to build advertising profiles, train AI models, or make automated decisions that affect your legal rights.

3. Third-Party Services

Supabase. We use Supabase (a cloud database and authentication provider) to store your account and run data securely. Supabase is hosted on AWS infrastructure in the United States. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

Apple / Sign in with Apple. Authentication is handled entirely by Apple. We never see or store your Apple ID password. Apple's privacy policy governs the sign-in flow: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Apple StoreKit / App Store. Subscription purchases (Run Fueled Pro, Run Fueled Pro + Integrations) are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Apple's App Store terms govern billing.

Resend (email delivery). Feedback submissions are forwarded to our internal inbox via Resend, a transactional email provider. Only the text of your submission and device info are transmitted — no health or run data is included.

We have no relationship with Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or any advertising network. None of those services are present in the App.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through the App (Settings → Delete Account), we permanently delete all of your data from our database within 30 days. Some data may persist in encrypted database backups for up to 90 days before those backups are overwritten; it cannot be accessed or restored after your deletion request.

If you sign out without deleting your account, your data remains stored so it can be restored when you sign back in.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Delete your account. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account within the App. This action is immediate and irreversible.

Access and correction. Your profile data (weight, fitness level, preferences) is editable at any time within the App's Settings screen.

Notification opt-out. You can disable all push notifications at any time through iOS Settings or within the App's notification preferences.

Cycle-phase tracking. This feature is off by default and can be disabled at any time. Disabling it stops collection and removes the field from future sync operations.

California residents (CCPA). We do not sell personal information. California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request deletion. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

European residents (GDPR). Our legal basis for processing your data is performance of a contract (delivering the service you signed up for). You have the right to access, rectify, erase, and port your data, and to object to or restrict processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

6. Children's Privacy

Run Fueled is not directed at children under 13 years of age (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's Keychain. Database rows are protected by Supabase Row Level Security policies — no user can access another user's data. We apply access controls limiting database access to the minimum necessary personnel.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we maintain industry-standard practices and will notify you of any breach as required by applicable law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
